# Communication & Sync Rhythm

As a distributed team across Malaysia and Indonesia, we rely heavily on effective communication. We balance synchronous alignment with asynchronous focus time to ensure maximum productivity.

## Synchronous Rhythm

We keep synchronous meetings short, focused, and actionable.

### Mon/Wed/Fri Standups (3:00 PM - 3:15 PM MYT)
We meet three times a week on Google Meet for a rapid, 15-minute alignment session. Each team member answers three questions:
1. **What did you accomplish since the last sync?**
2. **What are you focusing on today?**
3. **Are there any blockers in your way?**

*Note: These are not status-reporting meetings for management. They are peer-to-peer alignment sessions to resolve blockers quickly.*

### Bi-Weekly Planning & Retrospective (Monday, 3:00 PM - 4:00 PM MYT)
At the start of each two-week cycle (sprint), we meet to:
* Review the product roadmap and prioritize issues in Linear.
* Estimate tasks and commit to the sprint backlog.
* Reflect on what went well and what we can improve in our development process.

## Asynchronous Guidelines

Asynchronous communication is our default. It allows us to protect our deep-work focus time.

### 1. WhatsApp for Urgent Alerts
* **Use case:** Production outages, server crashes, urgent security alerts, or quick social catch-ups.
* **Etiquette:** Do not use WhatsApp for bug reports, design feedback, or complex task handovers. Use Linear instead.

### 2. Linear for Task-Specific Discussions
* **Use case:** Bug details, feature requirements, design mockups, and deployment logs.
* **Etiquette:** All technical decisions and code reviews must happen in Linear comments or GitHub Pull Requests. This ensures the history is preserved for future reference.

### 3. Respect Focus Time
* **Deep Work:** Engineers are encouraged to block out 4-hour windows daily for uninterrupted coding. Turn off notifications and set your status to "Focusing".
* **Response Time:** For non-urgent async messages, we expect a response within **4 hours** during working hours, or by the next morning if sent after hours.
