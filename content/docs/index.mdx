# Welcome to MyRehat

Welcome to the **MyRehat Engineering Handbook**. This handbook is our single source of truth for engineering practices, collaboration guidelines, infrastructure management, and deployment workflows.

We believe in public transparency, open-source-first alignment, and highly detailed asynchronous standard operating procedures. This handbook serves the same purpose as other industry-leading public handbooks: to align our distributed team, reduce communication overhead, and foster a culture of excellence.

## Our Philosophy

1. **Write It Down:** If it's not documented, it doesn't exist. All designs, decisions, and discussions must be written down in Linear or this handbook.
2. **Async-First:** We respect each other's time and focus. We communicate asynchronously by default, allowing team members to work in their zone of genius without constant interruptions.
3. **Automate Everything:** Manual steps are prone to human error. We automate testing, code quality checks, and deployments using modern CI/CD pipelines.
4. **Extreme Ownership:** Every engineer is a product owner. You are responsible for the code you write, from local development to production monitoring.

## Team Roles & Structure

MyRehat is a distributed team operating across Malaysia and Indonesia:

* **Chief Technology Officer (CTO):** Oversees architectural decisions, system stability, infrastructure security, and code quality. Located remote in Indonesia.
* **Chief Executive Officer (CEO):** Directs business growth, strategic partnerships, and product vision. Located in Malaysia.
* **Web Administrator:** Handles daily platform operations, content management, and user support. Located in Malaysia.
* **Software Engineers:** Build and maintain the core platform, AI tools, and custom plugins.
